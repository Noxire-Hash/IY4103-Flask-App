Archivist's Writs (AW)

The official currency of the Eternal Archive

The Archivist's Writs (AW) serve as a sacred shield, protecting both Seekers and Merchants within our realm. For Seekers, AW ensures your worldly currency is safely converted and stored, preventing unauthorized charges and protecting against fraudulent vendors. Your purchases are guaranteed by the Archive itself, with every transaction traceable and secure.

For our trusted Merchants, AW provides protection against chargeback fraud and payment disputes, ensuring their creations are fairly compensated. This system allows us to maintain swift and secure transactions while protecting the interests of all who trade within the Archive.

Think of AW as a mystical escrow, blessed by the LoreKeeper itself - where every transaction is verified, secured, and protected by the Archive's ancient magics.

Version System

Our version numbers follow the format: MAJOR.UPDATE.PATCH-STAGE

Number Format:
  • Major: Significant platform changes
  • Update: New features and improvements
  • Patch: Bug fixes and minor updates
Stage Indicators:
  • P (Preview): Exclusive testing version
  • L (Later): Near preview stage
  • E (Early): Early development
  • C (Current): Production version
